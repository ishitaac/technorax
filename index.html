ABOUT IEEE DAY

IEEE Day is celebrated to commemorate the time when brilliant engineers worldwide gathered to share their technical ideas in 1884. Every year, this fortunate day falls on 5 October 2022. The theme for IEEE day is “Leveraging Technology for a Better Tomorrow”. While the world benefits from what’s new, IEEE focuses on what’s next.

IEEE ADGITM celebrates this day with a twist by organizing one of the biggest flagship events called Technorax every year. This event contains both technical(hackathon) and non-technical competitions. Technorax is a two-day package full of opportunities, prizes, and fun. Contestants from all over India take part and make this the most successful event of IEEE ADGITM.

Themes

Education

One of the most affected sectors from this pandemic is education. The quality of education which was provided could have been a lot better. Keeping this in mind, under this theme, participants will have to come up with solutions and implement them to improve the existing education system in any way they think will help.

Healthcare

Healthcare always proved to be the most crucial aspect of a country's development. This pandemic was no different. That being said, under this theme, participants will have to think of solutions and implement them to revolutionize the modern healthcare system and solve real-world healthcare shortcomings.

Digital accessibility

Digital accessibility is the process of making digital products accessible to everyone. It is about providing all users access to the same information, regardless of any impairments that they may have. Come up with ideas and implement them in ways that can help improve the lives of people with disabilities.

Environment

The effects of deterioration of the environment and the urgency to protect it have always been alarming issues. In these desperate times, we need solutions fast. Leverage technology and come up with solutions that can help us in creating a more sustainable future.

Agriculture

Agriculture is the heart and soul of India. With the advancements in technology, agriculture is growing. With the same spirit, under this theme, participants will have to come up with ideas and implement them to revolutionize the agriculture sector.

Security

One of the biggest challenges for any country is security breaches. Keeping this in mind, under this theme, participants will have to come with solutions and code them to increase cyber security or anything they believe might benefit the security systems.

Open Innovation

IEEE is a platform where every idea is nurtured. Under this theme, you are allowed to come up with any innovative ideas and bring them to life. Build the ideas that you believe in and make a difference. No restrictions, no limitations! Let's spread the wings of imagination.
